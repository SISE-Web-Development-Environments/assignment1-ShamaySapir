My Story

My name is Roy Shamay.
I'm five and a half years old and I live in Be'er-Sheva with my parents Sapir and Ran Shamay.
My Hobbies are to run after balls, pull some ropes,
eat from the garbage when they don't pay attention and sleep 20 hours a day!
My first meeting with Sapir (Back then in 2015) was very stressful, I was only one and a half months old.
I was abandoned on the steps of the building by one of her neighbors.
I was really scared and so when she opened the door, I walked straight inside her apartment.
The minute she saw me she fell in-love.
When Ran came home, imagine his surprise, he was sure that Sapir is messing with him.
but the truth is that he really wanted me, too!
Fast forward to these days,
I'm so happy they have found me, and that I met them, I feel their love everyday.
